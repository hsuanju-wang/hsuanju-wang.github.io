ProFit - Clothes Recommendation System

Overview

ProFit - Your pro outfit. ProFit was my graduation project. It is a clothes recommendation system. There are two main functions. First, users can upload images of their clothes to create an online closet, and the system will recommend clothes in the closet based on what the user says. Second, users could upload images of the clothes and the system will find similar clothes in their closets or on the online stores.
